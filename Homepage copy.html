About Me


Hi! I'm Daniel Falzon.


I’m an artist and designer with a deep love for creativity in all its forms. I work across both digital and traditional mediums, blending classic techniques with modern tools to create art that tells a story. Whether it’s a pencil sketch, a digital painting, or a detailed design project, I put intention and emotion into every piece. Art has always been my way of exploring the world, expressing ideas, and connecting with others. I'm constantly experimenting, learning, and growing pushing the boundaries of my creativity and challenging myself with new projects. When I’m not creating, you can usually find me sketching in my notebook, hunting for inspiration, or enjoying the little things that spark imagination. Thanks for taking the time to visit I'm excited to share my work with you!
